Interested in running cross country next season but don't know who to ask questions of? Contact Coach Andy Edgar. My philosophy is if you are willing to put in the time and dedication, the more the merrier. Numbers = team strength. Not involved in a fall sport? Cross Country might just be the sport for you. Check us out!
